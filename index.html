About

My name is Marybeth Cave and I graduated in May 2022 with a Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) and Certificates in Archives and Digital Curation from the School of Information Studies (SOIS) program at Dominican University, located in River Forest, Illinois. My goal is to work with theatre and art archives to preserve their stories for future generations.

This website serves as my E-Portfolio, a requirement to graduate, and showcases my best graduate coursework. The following five learning goals are integrated into course curriculums:

1. Develop a professional identity and philosophy within the library and information professions.
2. Understand the essential nature of information and its relevance to our diverse society.
3. Navigate, curate, and create information across the spectrum of human records.
4. Synthesize theory and practice within a dynamic, evolving, and diverse information environment.
5. Effectively communicate and collaborate to deliver, market, and advocate for library and information services.

The navigation bar at the top of the page is divided into tabs for my resume, reflective essay, and the five learning goals.
